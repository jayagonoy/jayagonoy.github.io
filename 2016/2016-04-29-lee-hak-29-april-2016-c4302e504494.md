I heard that Lee Hak was not a buffet resto from the start, but when they converted to buffet, they are welcomed by a lot of people nearby. In addition, its proximity to Shaw Boulevard, Brgy. Kapitolyo and the Greenfield District (then called EDSA Central) makes it a good catch.

It’s actually my first time to see and taste meat grilled in _teppan_, and it’s good. I’ve also realized the goodness of South Korean ice cream in this place as well.

I didn’t know I was in for a good treat! — the next treat will be the time when I rest myself in bed.
