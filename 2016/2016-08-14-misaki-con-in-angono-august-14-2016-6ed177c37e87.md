**SM Center Angono — August 14, 2016**

It’s been a long time since I set foot in Angono, regarded as the country’s art capital. It’s also my first time to come to SM Center Angono to see **Misaki Con**. I took the opportunity as it’s held on a Sunday, and the traffic is light. One jeepney ride in an hour, and poof, I’m there.

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_tLBcuFpkEK7iXeiXmMu1nw.jpg)

The event had a good turnout of visitors even with the bad weather. I’ve heard thirty or so cosplayers who went there. [Arkadymac](http://www.arkadymac.com) may have more info on them than yours truly, so let’s wait for his post.
