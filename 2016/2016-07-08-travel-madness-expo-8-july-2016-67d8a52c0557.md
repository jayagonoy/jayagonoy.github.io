Last February, I went to the [Travel Tour Expo](https://jayagonoy.com/travel-tour-expo-2016-792a5913b09b/) at the SMX Manila. This July, I went to see the 5th **Travel Madness Expo** at the same spot. I think that’s something. Here are the photos from the event earlier.
