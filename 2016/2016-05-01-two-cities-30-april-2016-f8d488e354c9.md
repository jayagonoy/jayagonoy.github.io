## St. Paul University, Quezon City

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_f_da-9874qqZRlwZAH0hiA.jpg)

I was invited to see the final exams of one of my friends — which is a variety show — called “Be-Dazzled.” It sure have a good mix of segments, and I liked that one performance with a sax accompaniment (especially that, because we need some jazz for our soul).

The program is based on the good ol’ Hollywood during the times of Marilyn Monroe and so on, and while I did went out to get my anime itch off (I watched the latest Haifuri), overall, I enjoyed the show.

Congrats to its art director Hoshii — _malapit ka nang gumradweyt_.
