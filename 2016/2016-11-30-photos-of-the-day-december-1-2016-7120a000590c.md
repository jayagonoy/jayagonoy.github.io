Well, I’m finally here — Hi, Cebu!

I’m near the Fuente Circle at the moment, and I have yet to explore the rest of Cebu; but really, I’m in awe over my surroundings — I’m still wondering why newspapers have a different size than those of the daily Manila broadsheets.

I may not be able to sight-see as I’m here for work, but I am happy to be here for the first time.
