This year marks the first decade of the TAGCOM toys & Hobbies Convention organized by Hobby at Worx (formerly Hobbiworx) at the Megatrade Hall 2 of SM Megamall today.

Alongside this event is a bazaar and a shoe sale ([which have been the big thing there](https://www.facebook.com/gmanews/posts/10153887647311977)), but nevertheless the place has been calm throughout the day.
