### Throwback: Homura, on Haiku

“Hey, I miss this one!,” I said to myself when I went to my archives.

For this week’s throwback, I’m going to share a post circa April 2011, and at that time, one of the best series that I’ve watched called Puella Magi Madoka Magica was airing.

This is what I’ve thought Akemi Homura will write if she does a haiku about her relationship with protagonist Madoka Kaname.

* * *

> I was a failure.  
> I screw up a lot of times  
> I almost give up
> 
> Then one day I met  
> Someone that I could talk to  
> We became best friends
> 
> Then I found out that  
> She is a magical girl  
> She is powerful
> 
> I became one too  
> She teached me how to be one  
> I was happy then
> 
> All of a sudden  
> She died killing a monster  
> She has lost her life
> 
> I had turned back time  
> The same effects are still seen  
> I almost gave up
> 
> I wake up from this  
> I promise to protect her  
> Yes, no matter what
> 
> So here I am now  
> Calculated and Witty  
> I will protect her

* * *

As far as I remember, this is the last haiku I did in my life. I don’t see myself doing haiku in the future as well, but if I do, I’ll share it here.
