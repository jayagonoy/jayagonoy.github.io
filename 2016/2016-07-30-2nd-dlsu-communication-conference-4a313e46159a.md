I really waited for this day — and lack of sleep is no match for my renewed enthusiasm to come to one of my favorite refresher conferences.

**The 2nd DLSU Communication Conference** has expanded further, featuring more new and recent trends in quad media. I’ll be sharing a few tidbits from the sessions that I listened to during this whole-day activity.

In the morning plenary session, media pros such as Rappler’s **Maria Ressa**, indie filmmaker **Ellen Ongkeko-Marfil**, the Philippine Daily Inquirer’s **John Nery**, **David Guerrero** of BBDO Guerrero and ABS-CBN’s **JR dela Cerna**. Nery, whom I’ve been introduced first in the GMA Network broadcast of the PiliPinas Debates, did an experiment on Twitter prior to his talk, in which I got curious.
