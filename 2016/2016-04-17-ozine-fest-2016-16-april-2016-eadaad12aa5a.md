Negative comments aside, I was able to enjoy the first day of Ozine Fest 2016 yesterday. Why enjoy, when you have all of these terrible matters out of the blue? Simple: _I went there to hang out with friends, and not to write a report about it._ Well, I’m considering this as a report, but nah, I’m going to enjoy the day with my friends.
