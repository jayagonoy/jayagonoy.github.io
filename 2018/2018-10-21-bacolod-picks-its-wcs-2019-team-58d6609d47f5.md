Western Visayas representative performs Zenki

[![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/0_l8H8a4DHDpZ0zCQz.jpg)](https://www.facebook.com/visayascosplaysummit/photos/a.1216751685020831/2289542444408411/?type=3&permPage=1)

The First Leg for the World Cosplay Summit Philippines 2019 Qualifiers is successfully held at SM City Bacolod last Saturday, October 20.

Team Kazoku Saisei (composed of Kate Villarte and PJ Briones) is chosen as the representative for the Western Visayas qualifiers, cosplaying as Chiaki Enno and Zenki from Kishin Douji Zenki respectively.

Serialized in Shueisha’s Shonen Jump form 1992 to 1996, Kishin Douji Zenki is a story of the young priestess (_miko_) Chiaki Enno, and her journey to become the successor to the great Bodhisattva of Japan, Ozunu Enno.

The series aired on ABS-CBN in the 1990s. [Retro Pilipinas has more info](http://retropilipinas.blogspot.com/2012/05/zenki-abs-cbn-90s.html) about its popularity in the country.
