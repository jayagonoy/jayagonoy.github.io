Team Anime Core will be competing for the WCS PH title for the third time

Team Anime Core of Albay will once again take part in the National Qualifiers of the World Cosplay Summit Philippines as they have been selected as South Luzon’s competing pair.

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_kxqLd42PGdGOxEAf4Yv9rw.png)

The team of **John Albert Alejandrino** and **Jaypee Camu Azarcon** cosplaying characters from _Garo: The Animation_ has been selected among three competing teams during the South Luzon Qualifiers held at Pacific Mall Legazpi last Sunday, March 11.

# Join the WCSPH 2018 Qualifiers

The last qualifier for the World Cosplay Summit Philippines is set to be held [in Zamboanga on April 6](https://www.facebook.com/AmboxZamPen/videos/1785732844823445/), Friday.

The World Cosplay Summit Philippines National Qualifiers will be held at the Nippon Industrial and Cultural Exchange at the Almendras Gym in Davao City this April 29, 2018, Sunday.
