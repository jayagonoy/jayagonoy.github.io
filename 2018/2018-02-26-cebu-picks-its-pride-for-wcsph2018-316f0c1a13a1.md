Team Class S is the champion in the Central and Eastern Visayas Qualifiers of the World Cosplay Summit Philippines held at Otakufest 2018 inside Cebu Business Park last Saturday, February 24.

The team, composed of Seigfred Enero and Sheena Mae Butad, performed a skit as Aram and the Dark Lord of the hit game “Chain Chronicle.”

Judging the qualifiers are past WCS Philippine delegates [John Vincent Estapon](https://jayagonoy.com/we-take-a-look-at-john-vincent-estapon-who-is-among-the-first-delegates-of-the-philippines-to-the-617747c465f6) and [Lara Florentes](https://jayagonoy.com/zamboanga-team-selected-as-world-cosplay-summit-2017-ph-representative-ffcbb58aafbd), as well as Thai cosplayer Yuegene Fay, Korean cosplayer Eki Holic and WCS representative Ms. Chizuko Amano.

Otakufest 2018 Director for Production Boni Bryan Abuyog and Otakufest 2018 Chief Director Sonia Vidal signed the certificate. The competition is also featured in a TV Patrol Central Visayas report.

Prior to this, Team Class S has [showed its dominance](https://web.facebook.com/photo.php?fbid=10156677814719554&set=a.252043234553.180852.721539553&type=3) at Cosplay Mania 2017’s Ultimate Cosplay Championship, earning them the Grand Champion title.

https://www.facebook.com/joniorvisuals/videos/1970049256595719/

[Otakufest](http://www.facebook.com/ofest), established in 2007, is pioneered by the BAGSIK organization of the University of the Philippines Cebu, and was then placed under the management of the university’s Nichibunken organization on its 7th iteration. It is one of the city’s largest Cosplay and Hobby events, gathering 5,000 fans and counting.

# Join the WCSPH 2018 Qualifiers

The next qualifiers for the World Cosplay Summit Philippines is set to be held [in Bicol on March 11](https://www.facebook.com/TheOtakuBloodlineGuild/photos/a.212003758917593.45289.211994755585160/1557306397720649/?type=3), Sunday; [and in Zamboanga on April 6](https://www.facebook.com/AmboxZamPen/videos/1785732844823445/), Friday.

The World Cosplay Summit Philippines National Qualifiers will be held at the Nippon Industrial and Cultural Exchange at the Almendras Gym in Davao City this April 29, 2018, Sunday.
