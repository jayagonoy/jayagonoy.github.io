Team Twin Star and Team Phoenix selected last Sunday

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_q2OdKndnQ89fAWEkkLacCw.jpg)

Two teams have participated in the NCR Leg of the World Cosplay Summit Philippines 2019 Qualifiers held at the 13th Christmas Toys and Collectibles Fair at Megatrade Hall 2 of SM Megamall in Mandaluyong City on Sunday, December 16.

These two teams are Team Phoenix and Team Twin Star are both selected to represent the region.
