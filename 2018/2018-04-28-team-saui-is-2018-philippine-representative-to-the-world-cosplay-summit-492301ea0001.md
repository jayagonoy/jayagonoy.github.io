After years of waiting, it’s an NCR Qualifier winner who will represent the country — they are then called “Team Saui”

At this year’s World Cosplay Summit Philippines National Finals in Davao City held Sunday April 29, A deja vu has happened.

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_GpRziXnC61HrJBRUXMfBQw.jpg)

**Team Sekai** (then Team Saui, pronounced “Sa’yu ih”), the pair of Sergio Sta. Ana from Metro Manila and Kai Raito from Bacolod, will represent the country in the 2018 World Cosplay Summit which will happen at Nagoya, from July 28 to August 5.
