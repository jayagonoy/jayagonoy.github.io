Coslandia 2019 organizer HalloHallo Entertainment has released the set of activities for the three-day event, and I am here to give my impressions in each segment in the schedule.

Do take note that the schedule is subject to change, and I'll update this post once Coslandia has released an updated schedule online.

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/Coslandia-schedule.jpg)

## Anime and Manga

- **Anime Movie screenings** will be held on **all days** of Coslandia 2019 [in partnership with Pioneer Films](https://www.instagram.com/p/B3G6mNFBO-o/). Your Name., A Silent Voice and Your Lie in April are the titles set to be screened.
- A Live Manga Reading session is scheduled for Day 3.
- **[Voltes V](https://www.instagram.com/p/B2YZtRKh1eu/)** and **Mazinger Z**, two iconic anime titles of the 90s Generation, will have its spotlight on Day 3.

## Cute Characters

- Sanrio's **Hello Kitty and Dear Daniel** will be having a meet and greet on **Day 1**, while
- **Rilakkuma** will be meeting the guests of **Day 2**, and
- The iconic Pokemon **Pikachu** will be gracing Coslandia on **Day 3**.

## Cosplay and Fashion

- As announced, **[Cosplay Mode Magazine](https://www.instagram.com/p/B00CMn1Bn7Q/)** will be appearing at Coslandia 2019 - and they will have a guest cosplayer with them. This is aside with the [arrival of Nobuyuki Takahashi](https://www.instagram.com/p/B2v06ZSBicE/), the man who coined the term "Cosplay," to the country.
- Coslandia announced that [cosplayer **Miku 16**](https://www.instagram.com/p/B3L5YvphRRK/) will be in the country to grace the said event.
- **[Team Class S](https://www.instagram.com/p/B2Llu5hhzKR/)**, the 2019 Philippine representative to the **World Cosplay Summit**, will be gracing all days of Coslandia 2019. Aside from performing on stage, they will be sharing their experiences.
- For the first time, the **Pop Culture Hiroshima Philippines (PCHPH)** qualifiers [will be held for Luzon at Coslandia 2019](https://www.instagram.com/p/B23SXtXBBc0/). Before this, PCHPH held its first National Preliminaries which made Team Weapon and Mask Destruction of Davao its first country representative to the annual cultural exchange.
- **Solo Cosplay competitions** will also be held on days 1 and 3. **Cloth and Armor cosplays** are the focus of the competition on **Day 1**, while the **Best Hero and Heroine** shall take the spotlight on **Day 3**.
- A **[Cosplay Trick-or-Treat](https://www.instagram.com/p/B3EMC3Whw7u/)** for 4 to 12 years old will also be held on **Day 1**.
- Coslandia 2019 will also have its **Girls Collection Fashion Show** on **days 1 and 2**.

## Games and eSports

- **[MNL48 Janken Senbatsu](https://www.instagram.com/p/B0nhnvdhcWi/)**, which has been recently announced at Tokyo Game Show, will be featured at the event on **days 1 and 3**. MNLoves are expected to flock this new card game app, set to be available at Google Play and App Store soon.
- **Games** and **eSports** will also be a part of Coslandia 2019, to be held on **days 2 and 3**. Last year, it had a Mobile Legends tournament - I wonder what's their offering this year.

## Stage Performances

- Coslandia 2019 is all about Japanese artists, and the all-star lineup consists of:
    - **[TJ (Hirotoshi Maeda)](https://www.instagram.com/p/B124rEyhZCa/)** and **[Fairytales](https://www.instagram.com/p/B02kOxxhJTU/)** will be performing on **days 1 and 2**, while
    - **[Gracorex](https://www.instagram.com/p/B0nd8I2BntE/)**, **[1-Girls](https://www.instagram.com/p/B0nUftjhSi6/)**, **[Amatsusora](https://www.instagram.com/p/B2oAwc2B74k/)** and **[Samero](https://www.instagram.com/p/B0naPNmBLf5/)** will be performing on **Day 1**, and
    - **Tomoka** and **[Hazuki Tsubasa](https://www.instagram.com/p/B0nbIYZBQj7/)** will be performing on **Day 2**.
    - Let's not forget our proud local talent, Davao-based **[Pastel Mix](https://www.instagram.com/p/B2LnVOdh3_z/)**, who will be performing on **all days**!
- **MNL48** is set to reveal their [5th single](https://www.instagram.com/p/B0nOSs0BDJk/) with center girl **Gabb** in their **Concert** on **Day 2**. It is also announced that the 5th single will serve as the theme song of Coslandia 2019.

## Seikimatsu Blue

- Coslandia 2019 also announced the visit of **Seikimatsu Blue** author **[Shintaro Hirota](https://www.instagram.com/p/B00Lv1ZBlEp/)** and film director **[Tetsuya Hashi](https://www.instagram.com/p/B00M7SUBeej/)** along with the cast of the movie, a highlight of **Day 3**.

## Merchandise and other matters

- Limited edition English copies of **Seikimatsu Blue** comic book will be available at Coslandia 2019, ready to be signed by the cast and staff at the venue. Seikimatsu Blue will be a feature of **Day 3**.
- Back issues of **Cosplay Mode Magazine** will be made available, and is expected to release its English edition in the Philippines at Coslandia 2019, [according to the event's website](http://coslandia.wixsite.com/coslandia/gallery).
- Since we have mentioned **MNL48** here, their merchandise will also be available for purchase at Coslandia 2019 on **Day 3**.
- **[Creative Nation Academy](https://www.facebook.com/creativenationacademy/posts/1173848692807597)** [will also be at the event](https://www.facebook.com/creativenationacademy/posts/1173848692807597) to give live demonstrations of Adobe creative software "with a focus on animation and imaging."

**_Coslandia 2019_** _will be happening at SMX Convention Center Aura, SM Aura Premier, Taguig City on November 01 to 03 (Friday to Sunday). Tickets are available at SM Ticket outlets, through 470-2222 or online at SMTickets.com._

_Coslandia 2019 is presented in partnership with the Embassy of Japan in the Philippines, World Cosplay Summit Philippines and Pop Culture Hiroshima Philippines with participating sponsors_ _Brother Philippines_, _Pioneer Films, Creative Nation Academy and RapidShots360._

_More information on Coslandia 2019 are available on its website coslandia.com as well as its [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/CoslandiaPH/), [Twitter](https://twitter.com/CoslandiaPH) and [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/coslandiaph/) feeds._
