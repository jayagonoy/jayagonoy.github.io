Our friends from Aniplus has informed us that they will be airing the continuation of **Attack on Titan Season 3 Part 2** this April 29. This is Aniplus Asia’s first simulcast title for Spring 2019.

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_tSeiK5zt2BV-gAnonh2NWg.jpg)

If you played the video (you should), the squad is now in a quest to reclaim Wall Maria. That’s how far the story goes — of course, this is different from the manga’s progress, but let’s respect fellow anime casuals who are catching up to this series.

**New episodes will air every Monday starting April 29, 6:00 pm on Aniplus Asia.**

* * *

_In the Philippines, watch Aniplus on SKYcable SD Channel 139 in Metro Manila, Camanava/Rizal, Cavite, Laguna, Bulacan (San Jose Del Monte) via SKY Select for Php100 a month as well as Cablelink Channel 103 (Basic PLUS). If you have the EasyTV digibox from the guys at Solar, switch to Channel 10._
