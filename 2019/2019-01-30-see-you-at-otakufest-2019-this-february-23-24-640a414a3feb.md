I made sure that my second trip in Cebu will be in an otaku event — and not just any otaku event, it has to be the largest one.

I’ve been to three personal trips to explore the anime/cosplay culture — in Davao, in Singapore, and in Baguio.

Now, I’m going back to Cebu to witness one of its biggest events— Otakufest.

Last time, I featured Otakufest when it held its [World Cosplay Summit Philippines Preliminaries](https://jayagonoy.com/cebu-picks-its-pride-for-wcsph2018-316f0c1a13a1), and now I’m going to feature it again — this time, in person.

https://www.facebook.com/ofest/posts/1501606909943357

Otakufest started as an event held inside UP Cebu and has grown to be one of the city’s largest Cosplay and Hobbies Convention-in fact, they gathered 5,000 visitors in 2017!

Held every February, it has become an opening event for anime/cosplay fans and hobbyists. ([Last year, they got Bless4 to perform there](https://weekend.sunstar.com.ph/blog/2018/03/25/bless4-brings-cebu-together-warriors-spirit-otakufest-2018/).)

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_EypD-BHZVGOfX6QqZxCXJw.png)

# #2daysofOF

If you noticed their [website](http://www.facebook.com/ofest), they have the whole story and the milestones there, but this year’s Otakufest will be different. Those who got used to attending it know that it’s held in just one day, but how about if they held it for two days?

https://www.facebook.com/ofest/posts/1853320001438711

## World Cosplay Summit Philippines (WCSPH) 2019 Cebu Preliminaries

As part of my ongoing tradition of taking a peek into the developments of the World Cosplay Summit in the Philippines, I’ll be featuring its 2019 Cebu Preliminaries wherein two teams will represent Cebu in the Nationals coming soon.

## Pop Culture Hiroshima Philippines Qualifiers & National Finals

I’ve noticed that Japan’s Pop Culture Hiroshima (PCH) has a foothold in the Philippines after partnering with Ambox Events (which handles WCSPH activities in Cebu and Davao), but to have a National Finals already? Time sure flies fast.

On this part, I’ll be checking out the story of PCH which also opened its doors to countries such as Thailand, Indonesia and India.

WCSPH 2018 Team Philippines [Team Sekai](https://jayagonoy.com/team-saui-is-2018-philippine-representative-to-the-world-cosplay-summit-492301ea0001) as well as Reika will be gracing the event. Reika will even have her own meet and greet session there.

https://www.facebook.com/ofest/posts/1845039242266787

## “One Piece” Day

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/0_E-WanrWSYUaAtmQP.jpg)

Speaking of WCSPH, manga behemoth Shueisha partnered with the World Cosplay Summit to hold the One Piece Cosplay Grand Prix in line with the 10th year anniversary celebration of the series’ first TV broadcast in Japan.

Among the activities are trivia contests, a One Piece showcase and a cosplay competition exclusive for those who will come to Otakufest’s second day as characters from the show. Notable Cebuana cosplayer and game streamer Miles “Mais” Semblante will be hosting this event.(See the complete list of activities [in this post](https://web.facebook.com/ofest/photos/a.422232114547514/1863026280468083/).)
