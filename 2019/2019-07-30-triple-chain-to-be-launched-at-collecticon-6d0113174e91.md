Attention to those who already have the game installed on their smartphones — read this announcement!

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/0_QUlVHGbJy8B6kvru.jpg)

Gamers of all ages can enjoy this Strategy and Puzzle RPG game, which redefines the genre through its challenging and addictive gameplay:

- **Story Modes and Challenging Maps** lets you conquer quests and map challenges with your team of heroes.
- **Strategic Gameplay** lets you create a strategic chain of explosive combos and high-damage attacks.
- **6 Unique Hero Classes** — Warrior, Mage, Healer, Tanker, Specialist, and Dancer — has its own specialization on the battlefield.

- From those six classes where you can choose a **wide selection of heroes** — more than 25 heroes await for you to choose them for your team.
- There’s also a Real-time PvP option where you can top rank and challenge your friends.

Collecticon guests who has Triple Chain already installed on their smartphones stand a chance to win prizes — show your Triple Chain app at the Cubizone booth inside Collecticon 2019 at Megatrade Hall 3 inside SM Megamall in Mandaluyong City from September 14 to 15, 2019.
