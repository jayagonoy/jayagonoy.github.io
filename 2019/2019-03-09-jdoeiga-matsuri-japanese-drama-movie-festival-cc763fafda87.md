### Philippine Fan gathering to celebrate Japanese drama & movies

The first time I got to a **JDoramaEiga PH** event is when they held a block screening of _Itoshi no Irene_ at Robinsons Galleria as part of the QCinema Film Festival last year. Guess what: They even got the lead Cebuana actress Nats Sitoy to join in the screening, and a Q&A was done afterwards.

Now, they sent me an email telling me more about their first fan gathering for this year, **JDoEiga Matsuri** which will happen this April.

They have a lot of activities in store, as follows:

> **JDoEiga Matsuri** aims to highlight the overflowing love and passion of Filipino fans for Japanese live-action visual media culture through fan booths, contests and character cosplay.

> Guests can expect fun and festive activities such as **JDoEiga Uta Nodojiman**, a singing contest featuring Japanese drama and live-action theme songs;

> **Koi Dance Challenge**, a dance contest showcasing the hit song “Koi” (Love) by Gen Hoshino from the Japanese drama We Married as a Job (popularly known as NigeHaji);

> **JDoEiga Cosplay Party** where participants can dress up as their favorite Japanese drama or movie characters;

> and **JDoramaEiga Quiz Bee**, where fans can test their knowledge on the classic and latest Japanese dramas and movies.

> **Various fan clubs, Japanese culture-related merchants, and sponsors** will be actively participating in this one-of-a-kind event, which will also feature special performances from esteemed guest contest judges.

_The first JDoEiga Matsuri will be held on April 7, 2019, 10:00 AM to 7:00 PM at VentureSpace PH, 2/F CBC Corporate Center, 724 Shaw Boulevard, Mandaluyong City (This is just in front of Worldwide Corporate Center, and a walking distance from Starmall EDSA-Shaw)._

_For more information,_ [_visit the event on Facebook_](https://bit.ly/JDoEigaMatsuri)_, email_ [_jdoramaeigaph@gmail.com_](mailto:jdoramaeigaph@gmail.com) _or text 09772569879._
