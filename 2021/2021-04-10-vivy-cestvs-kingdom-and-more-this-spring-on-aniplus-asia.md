_Aniplus Asia has released its lineup of anime titles for its Spring 2021 broadcast. BACK ARROW continues its broadcast run on the channel as well._

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/Vivy-Cestvs-Kingdom-and-more-this-Spring-on-Aniplus-Asia.jpg)

Aniplus Asia continues to bring anime this Spring 2021 anime season, and just as with other releases from the Southeast Asian channel, I'll be chiming in my thoughts in brief. Let's go!

_All times are on GMT+8 AM/PM format, 24-hour format means next day broadcast. Broadcast times may change._

## New Shows

### **Vivy -Fluorite Eye’s Song-**

****To bring happiness with my song—this is my mission.****

https://youtu.be/tIy8mdQ92ls

New Episodes every Saturdays 24:00 (Sundays 12:00am) / Encores Every Sundays 8:30pm

> _NiaLand is an A.I. theme park that blends dreams with hope and science. Vivy was created as the world’s first autonomous humanoid A.I. to work as a cast member in the theme park. She gets on stage every day to sing, but she is not very popular._
> 
> _“I want my singing to bring happiness to everyone.”_
> 
> _Vivy continues singing in hopes of one day achieving her goal of performing on the theme park’s main stage._
> 
> _One day, an A.I. who calls himself Matsumoto appears before her. Matsumoto claims to be an A.I. from one hundred years into the future, and that his mission is to rewrite history with Vivy, in order to stop a war between A.I. and humans that would happen a century later._
> 
> _How will the encounter between these two A.I.s with such different missions change the course of the future?_
> 
> _This is a story of I (Vivy) destroying I (A.I.)..._
> 
> _The one-hundred-year journey of an A.I. songstress named Vivy starts now._

I've been seeing the name Atsumi Tanezaki these days - she has three roles this season, and one of it is Vivy. Looking at the trailer alone, I'm hyped as heck! Aside from Tanezaki, we have THE Jun Fukuyama here.

The question is, how long will this series be? Only one way to find out.

### ****CESTVS -The Roman Fighter-****

**This is the story of a kind-hearted boy's battle against his cruel fate.**

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=\_PFD\_l0y6E4

Premieres **15 April**, Every Thursdays 1:30am / Encores on Thursdays 6:00pm

> 54 AD, the Roman Empire. Cestvs, a young slave who was placed in a school for boxers takes on his very first match, where he is matched against a good friend of his. Despite some hesitation, he wins the fight as the loser is killed on the spot.
> 
> Tomorrow will not come for those who lose.
> 
> "This is no different from murder if my opponent has to die each time I win!"
> 
> Clutching onto the sorrow and rage within his fists, Cestvs fights to survive as he faces powerful opponents one after another while the coronation of the Fifth Roman Emperor, Nero, takes place.
> 
> With no other chance at freedom other than to keep winning, how will Cestvs seize his future?!

If I understood this correctly, this adaptation of a manga series is about boxing in the Roman Empire. I can feel the shonen vibe in this one.

### ****Kingdom (Season 3)****

**We will not falter.**

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=\_DRuqT2cVFU

New Episodes Every Mondays 3:00am / Encores Every Mondays 6:00pm

> A nameless boy and a young king have grown up in a nation plagued by war. The boy, Xin, has countlessly proven himself on the battlefield, and although initially starting on bad terms, he and the young king, Zheng, have become comrades.
> 
> Zheng wishes to bring all the warring states under Qin, while Xin climbs the ranks to become a Great General. Both of their ambitions will change history forever.
> 
> The third season is set after the successful Shanyang campaign, as varying sides continue to suffer in the face of war.
> 
> The Qin army is a step closer to unifying China. Meanwhile, Li Mu of Zhao takes advantage of the temporary truce with Qin to negotiate with the other states. Realizing it too late, Qin finds the armies from the 6 states at its doorstep...

Kingdom has been broadcast from 2012 to 2013, followed by a sequel from 2013 to 2014, and after quite a long time, the series returns for another season.

### **CARDFIGHT!! VANGUARD overDress**

**Welcome to the new world of Vanguard.**

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=iuauvCCrPqA

New Episodes Every Saturdays 6:00pm / Encores Every Sundays 5:00pm

> Third-year middle school student Yu-yu Kondo lives in Kanazawa city of the country of Kaga.
> 
> Yu-yu has the ability to read the heart of others and is troubled by the fact that he is unable to reject requests. Often getting caught up in his sister’s hobbies, Yu-yu could no longer take it and runs away from home. He is saved by Megumi Okura, who then invites him to a night amusement park called "Wonder Hill", where her friends gather.
> 
> At the amusement park, Yu-yu meets Megumi, along with Zakusa Ishigame and Tomari Seto—members of "Team Blackout" who gather to play Vanguard every night.
> 
> Tonight, a serious fight with their team flag on the line will be held. Blackout’s Leader, Danji Momoyama, will fight against the mysterious powerhouse fighter, Tohya Ebata. The cardfight gradually transforms into a scene on Planet Cray before Yu-yu’s eyes.
> 
> This is how Yu-yu encounters Vanguard, and is drawn in by the appeal of the world and friends he has never seen before.

With character designs from CLAMP, and Aoi Shouta topbilling the voice cast, this really IS a new kind of Vanguard.

## Continuing Series

### **BACK ARROW**

**The search for his memories beyond the Wall begins**

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FGuwEhBsE4U

New Episodes Every Fridays 24:30 (Saturdays 12:30am) / Encores Every Saturdays 8:00pm

> Lingalind is a land surrounded by the Wall. The Wall encircles, protects, cultivates, and nurtures the land. The Wall is God—and that, is the root of the land of Lingalind.
> 
> One day, a mysterious man named Back Arrow appears in Edger Village which is located at the outskirts of Lingalind. Arrow lost all his memories, but claims to have "come from the outside of the Wall".
> 
> To regain his memories, Arrow sets out to go beyond the wall, but is embroiled in a conflict that revolves around himself.

BACK ARROW continues to broadcast on the channel. I've read somewhere that the show's picking up steam with its latest episode.
