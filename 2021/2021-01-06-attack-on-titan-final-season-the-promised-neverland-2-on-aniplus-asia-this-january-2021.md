_"Bottom-Tier Character Tomozaki," "2.43 Seiin High School Boys Volleyball Team," "Back Arrow," _"The Irregular at Magic High School" Season 1_ to also premiere this January; "D4DJ First Mix" continues_

As part of its New Year salvo, ANIPLUS Asia will air three new series from the 2021 Winter season. Let's check out what ANIPLUS Asia has to offer.

_All times are on GMT+8 AM/PM format, 24-hour format means next day broadcast. Broadcast times may change._

## New Shows

### Bottom-tier Character Tomozaki

_**Conquering the game of life, one level at a time**_

https://youtu.be/DTyYETL1NjA

**Premieres 8 January** / New Episodes on Fridays, 8:30pm / Encores on Saturdays, 6:30pm

> Fumiya Tomozaki is one of the top-gamers in Japan, and a loner at high school who treats life as a "crappy game".
> 
> One fateful day, he meets Aoi Hinami, the school's "perfect heroine".
> 
> "This game you call 'life'—take it head on!"
> 
> Will life turn out to be a crappy game, or a god-tier game?
> 
> With Hinami's guidance, the bottom-tier high-schooler begins his journey to conquer the game of life!

As one of the top light novels in the "Kono Light Novel ga Sugoi!" rankings for four consecutive years, this show already has a promise to perk up the ones who need a "Guide to Life."

If [Saekano](https://jayagonoy.com/podcast-83-saekano-movie/)'s ploy is to "raise a boring girlfriend," this one has an ultimate goal: "Become a normie like me." By this time, we can stop hating on riajuu.

### BACK ARROW

**_From the creator of Code Geass and writer behind Kill La Kill_**

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/Aniplus-BACK-ARROW.jpg)

**Premieres 8 January** / New Episodes every Friday, 24:30 (Saturdays 12:30am) / Encores on Saturdays, 8:00pm

> Lingalind is a land surrounded by the Wall. The Wall encircles, protects, cultivates, and nurtures the land. The Wall is God — and that, is the foundation of the land of Lingalind.
> 
> One day, a mysterious man named Back Arrow appears in Edger Village which is located at the outskirts of Lingalind. Arrow lost all his memories, but claims to have "come from the outside of the Wall".
> 
> To regain his memories, Arrow sets out to go beyond the wall, but is embroiled in a conflict that revolves around himself.

The name of the main character is literally "Back Arrow," but it can be mispronounced as "baka yarō" (idiot) depending on how he was able to penetrate the wall outta nowhere.

### 2.43 Seiin High School Boys Volleyball Team

_**Teamwork makes the dream work; aim for the top**_

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/2-43-Seiin-High-School-Boys-Volleyball-Team.jpg)

**Premieres 8 January**, 2:10am / New Episodes every Fridays, 1:30am / Encores on Fridays, 6:30pm (\*Episodes 2 onwards will air earlier.)

> After causing huge trouble at the powerhouse volleyball team in his junior high school in Tokyo, Kimichika Haijima moves back to his hometown, Fukui. There, he is reunited with his childhood friend, Yuni Kuroba.
> 
> Kuroba possesses outstanding physical abilities but crumbles under pressure, while Haijima, with his overwhelming passion and talent for volleyball, often locks horns with those around him. While the two excel as a formidable duo, their relationship sours as they part ways after their final Prefectural tournament as middle school students. Despite that, they both enroll at Seiin High School, where they join the boys volleyball team.
> 
> Welcoming them to the team are the third-year pair between Oda, the 163cm-tall hot-blooded captain, and Aoki, the talented but sharp-tongued vice-captain, and second-year students, including Kanno, who has sun allergy, and Ookuma, a beginner to volleyball.
> 
> The passionate story about the minor volleyball team in Fukui aiming for the nationals begins!
