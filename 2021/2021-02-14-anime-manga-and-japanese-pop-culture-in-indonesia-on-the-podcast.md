On this special two-hour [podcast](/podcast) episode: I spoke to my good friends in Indonesia, Halimun Muhammad (Senior Editor at [KAORI Nusantara](http://kaorinusantara.or.id)'s [The Indonesian Anime Times](http://kaorinusantara.or.id/english)) and [Luthfi Suryanda Atmojo](https://twitter.com/luthficantweet) (Podcaster, [LUTALK](https://open.spotify.com/show/2Pkv4GbecCiPFp6pf1wXf5?si=UYUrY1XwRP6i9xbJMn44Ww&nd=1) / Collaboration Manager, [Ghosty's Comic](https://www.facebook.com/GHOSTY.COMIC/)) to discuss Anime, Manga and Japanese Pop Culture in Indonesia.

I've been talking about [Indonesia](https://jayagonoy.com/are-the-philippine-and-indonesian-otaku-communities-similar-9bfce8dcd811/) on the blog before, so this is a great opportunity and a step forward towards more interaction with one of the largest consumers of Japanese pop culture in Southeast Asia.
