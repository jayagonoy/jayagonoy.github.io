Interacting with [Virtual YouTubers](/vtubers) is fun, especially if both of you are interested in one or more topics. On this episode, I got Virtual YouTuber [Terumi Koizumi](https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCSmWTq8TP7zejNS6qbJLHUw) talk about branding on this hour-long episode.

Given that we mentioned a lot of references, it's much more appropriate to include the show notes for this episode here. We talked about a lot of brands, mostly local in nature.

Then the rest of the discussion is allotted to various topics like television broadcasting - which reminds me of [her brutal tweet](https://twitter.com/TerumiKoizumi/status/1398603892779225090).
