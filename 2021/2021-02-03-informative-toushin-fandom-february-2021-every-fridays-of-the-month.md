It's been a long time since I saw a local event related to the anime culture, so when [I saw this on my Facebook news feed](https://www.facebook.com/toushin.org/posts/2916591648613356), I didn't hesitated to sign up. At all. Especially if it's about VTubers.

Bias aside, this is a good way to start weekends.

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/Toushin-Fandom-February.jpg)

**Ateneo de Manila University's Touyou no Shinju (Toushin) Organization** will hold its "Fandom February" talks featuring relevant topics such as VTubers, Gundam, UTAU, Isekai, Idol Culture and 3D Modelling.

<figure>

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/Toushin-Fandom-February-Schedule-1.jpg)

<figcaption>

[Image from Toushin on Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/toushin.org/posts/2914933705445817)

</figcaption>

</figure>

Those who are interested to join can register via [https://toushinfanfeb.webflow.io](https://toushinfanfeb.webflow.io/). The talks will also be streamed live on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/toushin.org) and [Twitch](https://www.twitch.tv/toushinorg).
