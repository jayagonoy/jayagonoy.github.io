![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/Final-Fantasy-Fans-to-Celebrate-34th-Anniversary-online.jpg)

Squeenix Productions, a group composed of fans of Square Enix games, will celebrate the 34th Anniversary of Final Fantasy series online this December 18, Saturday.

The Virtual FF 34th Anniversary Party will be streamed live on Facebook from 8pm to 12 midnight Philippine Time. This 4-hour live stream will feature game tournaments, a fund-raising auction and a virtual concert.

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/Virtual-FF34th-Lineup.jpg)

There will also be a live cosplay meet for fans and cosplayers alike. More details are posted by the organizer [Squeenix Productions on Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/squeenixheroes/).
