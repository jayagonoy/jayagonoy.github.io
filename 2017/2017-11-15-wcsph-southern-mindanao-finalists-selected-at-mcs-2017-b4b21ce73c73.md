**Three teams to proceed to National Qualifiers—including a Wildcard finalist**

The search for the next Philippine representative for the 2018 World Cosplay Summit in Nagoya, Japan continues at the Mindanao Cosplay Summit organized by Ambox Events at the Main Mall Atrium of SM City Davao.

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_4lNym0VQ0IuFY3BYnrQqxQ.png)

In the said qualifier, three teams are selected to represent Southern Mindanao — **Team Cookie Moonstar**, **Team Tribe**, and the wildcard entry **Team Riders of the Grand Order**.

https://www.facebook.com/Ambox.Events/posts/1796291090389599

# Join the WCSPH 2018 Qualifiers

The next set of qualifiers for the World Cosplay Summit Philippines will be at the Christmas Toys & Collectibles Fair of the Asian Comics, Toys and Hobbies Collective to be held at the Megatrade Hall 2 of SM Megamall this December.
