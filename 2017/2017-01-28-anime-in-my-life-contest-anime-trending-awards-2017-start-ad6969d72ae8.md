## Aniplus’ “Anime in my Life” contest 2017 announced

In case you missed this: **Aniplus** has announced its winner of their “**Anime in my Life**” contest, wherein anime fans submit their content to the channel related to how Anime revolves around their lives.

<figure>

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_1LpfY8tqWZnM64hH8dNJSg.jpg)

<figcaption>

The winners for the Burst 4 of the Aniplus “Anime in my Life” contest — including the grand winner Rei Lim who will be visiting AnimeJapan 2017 courtesy of the channel ([watch the announcement here](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=GaaEoskSlfs)).

</figcaption>

</figure>

They also announced that the 2017 contest is now open for submissions. Tell Aniplus how is anime a part of your life through any of the four forms:

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_Z860ROSbAQq2vN1L1hbP3g.jpg)

- **DRAW**: “Be it a pencil or a stylus, show us your artistic skills as you re-create the character of your choice or create a new character! All forms of fanart are welcome!”
- **FILM**: “Showcase your talents through song, instrument or dance and record your moment in the spotlight. Or you could simply do a room tour and impress us with your dedication to anime!”
- **SNAP**: “Photograph an image that captures your love for anime! From your proud figurine collection to your cool cosplay moment. As long as your photograph is related to anime, you’re good to go!”
- **WRITE**: “Have many things to say about your favourite anime but there’s no platform to express your thoughts? Show us your literary flair for writing by telling us why you love anime and how it has influenced your life.”

The submission periods (bursts) are as follows:

- **1st Burst**: 20 Jan — 31 May 2017
- **2nd Burst**: 1 June — 15 August 2017
- **3rd Burst**: 16 August — 1 November 2017

_NOTE: In each burst, there will be ONE Top Prize and FIVE Runner Up winners selected, and top entries from all bursts (including all FILM entries) will be competing for the Grand Prize:_

⦁ Grand Prize A: Burst 1 — **Trip to experience Attack on Titan: The Real** at Universal Studio Japan

⦁ Grand Prize B: Burst 2 & 3 — **VIP Access to Anime Festival Asia Singapore**

Both are inclusive of a pair of admission tickets, air tickets & 4 days 3 nights accommodation.

You must be thinking about planning what to submit as you read this.

_(Note: Aniplus sends me information about their broadcasts and promotions.)_
