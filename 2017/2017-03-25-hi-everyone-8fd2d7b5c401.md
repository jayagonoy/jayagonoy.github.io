_Hi everyone! This week’s photo set is all about Ikebanas. You know, those intricate flower arrangements that help beautify a living space._

There was this exhibit by the Ikebana International Manila Chapter 108 in commemoration of its golden year of establishment.

The exhibit was held at the Glorietta’s Palm Drive Activity Center (the more modest one) from March 10 to 12, and while I missed out on a lot, below are jut a few snaps from the said area.

Coming from a perspective of someone who is not interested in Ikebana enough to appreciate each intricacies, I can say few; but I’m sure the Ikebana International chapter’s members will be sharing a lot of stories — they even had an Ikebana artist demonstration around those dates.
