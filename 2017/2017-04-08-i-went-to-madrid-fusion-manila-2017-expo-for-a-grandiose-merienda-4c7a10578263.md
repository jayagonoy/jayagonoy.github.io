- There’s this gelato shop called “[Manila Creamery](https://www.facebook.com/ManilaCreamery/)” (they have a branch at the UP Town Center)which serves Burnt rice gelato (as in “Tinutong na Kanin”). I promised them I’ll go there to eat a full scoop.
- Sweetened cacoo nibs from Sikwate! are the best — I think the name is familiar to me though.
- My favorite Mindanaoan snack/bread Bulua is also visible in the Expo!
- Nestle Food Services is generous with its offerings — too bad I was only able to get a sample of their soft-serve concoction.
- There are other exhibits outside the Expo — there’s this Coconut Chips in salted and sweetened variations that are perfect for dressing desserts (or just plain snacking).
- What I like about the Expo is that I am able to taste a lot — no bullcrap, it’s like I went there for a grandiose _merienda_. Maybe the event’s not as grandiose unlike last year, but it’s still grandiose. Still looking forward to the next offering.

_Disclaimer: I got the invite from my boss who is managing the event’s security, same as last year’s. Many thanks, boss!_
