**Pinoy Rig Enthusiasts releases Limited Edition Gaming Chairs — Pre-orders Now Available**

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_LCC6tjgFeEPD9Si_MlWPqw.jpg)

**Alvin Pacheco of Pinoy Rig Enthusiasts** told me via email that they will be selling limited edition gaming chairs in collaboration with Glaiiide from Aneno Knights.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=rGo0kUFAgbc

Alvin describes the chair as one “With an Ergonomic Racing style Gaming Chair that has a neck pillow and lumbar support, \[…\] The chair includes a reclineable backrest and a vertically adjustable arm support.”

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_eFQx1Szn2udeiopHerIKWw.png)

The chair is made from high-grade materials, has a 1.8mm thick metal frame, 19.89mm diameter, Class-4 gas lift that can support up to 200kg weight, breathable leather, and a 42cm seat width, said to be “wider than normal seats.”

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_AZyiiN5Qs_rQ0pmtgrc6yw.jpg)

Only 9 seats will be available for pre-ordering, and discounts are up for the first 5 buyers. “The price of the PRE Gaming Chair is Php 8,000 pesos for the first 5 pre-orders, and Php 9,000 pesos for the last 4 pre-orders. The chair has a 12 months warranty package, and the buyer also has the option to replace it with a new stock or refund the whole amount with an additional Php 100 for compensation within 2 months after receiving the chair.”

Pre-orders for the PRE Gaming Chair can be done through [this form](https://goo.gl/forms/BT6m9sbchKmX6y6o1).

For those who don’t know PRE, they’ve been doing reviews on PC gaming rigs in the Filipino language. You should check out their [YouTube](http://www.youtube.com/PinoyRigEnthusiasts), [Facebook](http://www.facebook.com/PinoyRigEnthusiasts) and [Twitter](http://www.twitter.com/PinoyRigEnths) for updates.
