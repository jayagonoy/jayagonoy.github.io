**If you want to get a glimpse of ToyCon, here’s your chance**

The ToyCon x PopLife FanExperience wil be held on the weekend of June 30 to July 2 at SMX Manila — but before that, a Launch Event will be held at the Uptown Mall inside the Bonifacio Global City starting now until Sunday.

Last year, there was no ToyCon Launch event, so I am happy that they will have one this year — and of course, they will have a press conference with it.

If you or you have someone who wants to see ToyCon but can’t afford to go to the actual event yet, tell them to go there. Uptown Mall (not to be confused with the one in Katipunan) is a jeepney ride away from the Guadalupe MRT station passing through the BGC.
