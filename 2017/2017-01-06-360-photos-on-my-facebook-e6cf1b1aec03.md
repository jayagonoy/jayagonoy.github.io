If you might have noticed, **I’ve been tinkering with 360 photos on Facebook**. I’ve just posted my second photo there.

There are two things that are important in making these photos:

1. The size of the photo, and
2. The metadata.

Luckily, Facebook has [very helpful resources](https://web.archive.org/web/20220317212421/https://facebook360.fb.com/editing-360-photos-injecting-metadata/) on how to make ones from your Photoshop or GIMP. Best of all, it’s very easy — so easy that even meme pages can create their own set.
