**A brief backgrounder on my watchlist**

_Alrighty guys — here’s some of the series that I am watching after work, because anime is life. Let me know if you can guess my taste in anime for this season by responding here on Medium. Catch you all later!_

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_w9WfMiqxotlSsSJ33BgocQ.jpg)

[**Ren’ai Bou-kun**](http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/anime.php?id=18906) has a catchy opening theme (thank you Wake Up, Girls!), has a [Nunu](http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=81503) and [Harami](http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=98448) in it, and [Yoppi](http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=123095) has her own first main character debut aside from WUG and Scorching Ping Pong Girls. Its first few moments are enjoyable and funny, but I am afraid that the story might turn bland and repetitive — let’s see how long I will go for this.

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_UkWMAp4tZcWMIg2D-yCoQ.jpg)

**Sakura Quest**, P.A.Works’ latest series is a must-watch. There’s [Ueshama](http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=116390), Chika Anzai, [Moyochi](http://www.animenewsnetwork.com/encyclopedia/people.php?id=86495) (mmmm Eupho — , _dejoke_), and Vinay Murthy (who in 2010 did a stand-up comedy skit that is still relevant today and will be relevant forever unless Japan pop changed its mind). Now, the question is this — will the mini-kingdom’s previous visitor be successful in revitalizing it?

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_GSLfJbPBP4zF42CPOK_ZEQ.jpg)

The World Cosplay Summit anime **ID-0**, while in CG (I watched Kemono Friends which is also a CG anime so you’d better think twice on hitting on that aspect), has an interesting first episode. I plan to stick on this one as well. (I hereby solemnly swear that Ari Ozawa has an odd role here).
