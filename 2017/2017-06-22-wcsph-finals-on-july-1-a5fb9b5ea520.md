### World Cosplay Summit Philippines National Finals at ToyCon 2017 on July 1

[![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_lPbcT6YZHheNexSV4FtUxg.jpg)](https://www.facebook.com/wcsph/photos/a.470531263026912.1073741828.441194635960575/1428866320526730/?type=3&theater)

A quick reminder to all — the World Cosplay Summit Philippines National Finals (#WCSPH) will be held at ToyCon 2017 this July 1, Saturday. Aside from the competitors from Manila, Bicol and Davao, we now have competitors from Bacolod (selected during the Ambox’s Visayas Cosplay Summit qualifiers).

To recap — In Manila, two pairs are selected by default in December last year. \[Read more: [https://jayagonoy.com/team-reality-team-kanor-cubes-to-represent-ncr-in-wcsph-2017-ac95f158e0a3](https://jayagonoy.com/team-reality-team-kanor-cubes-to-represent-ncr-in-wcsph-2017-ac95f158e0a3)\]

The last National finals in Metro Manila was held in 2015, during the Hero Face Off competition as part of the cable channel’s 10th year. After that, the National Finals was held in Mindanao. \[Read more: [https://jayagonoy.com/the-2015-world-cosplay-summit-philippines-national-finals-9dd02fde6081](https://jayagonoy.com/the-2015-world-cosplay-summit-philippines-national-finals-9dd02fde6081)\]
