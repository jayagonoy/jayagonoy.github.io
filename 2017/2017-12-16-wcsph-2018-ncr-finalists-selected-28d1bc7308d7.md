**Two teams who competed at the 12th Christmas Toys and Collectibles Fair will proceed to Davao for the Nationals this April 2018**

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_OQVVGQsXYdXI0cOlFnZfAw.png)

Two teams are added to the set of finalists vying for the right to represent the Philippines in the World Cosplay Summit in August 2018, both coming from the NCR Qualifiers held at the last day of the 12th Christmas Toys and Collectibles Fair (December 17, 2017, Sunday) at the SM Megatrade Hall 2 in Mandaluyong City.

<figure>

![](https://jayagonoy.com/wp-content/uploads/1_Rcb-CVGWFdOC-m4A9IvcDg.jpg)

<figcaption>

Team Deathmatch and Team Saui with Furomi Nomura of the WCSPH Committee

</figcaption>

</figure>

**Team Deathmatch** represented its fellow Monster Hunter players by sharing their gaming experience in a skit, while a duo coming respectively from Manila and Bacolod formed Team Saui and did a skit from Sengoku Basara.

Select members of the Toycon Philippines organizing team which is the host to the said qualifier served as judges.

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=2tgA0snmt0I

**Team Saui** is announced as champion, but the WCSPH Committee has decided to pick both teams to represent the NCR in the National Qualifiers to be held at the Nippon Industrial and Cultural Exchange in Davao City on Sunday, April 29, 2018.

* * *

# Join the WCSPH 2018 Qualifiers

The next set of qualifiers for the World Cosplay Summit Philippines will be held next year in Bicol (at the Pacific Mall Legazpi on January 18, 2018), Zamboanga (at Matsuri Fever, scheduled on March 2018) and Cebu (at the UP Otakufest this February 24, 2018).
