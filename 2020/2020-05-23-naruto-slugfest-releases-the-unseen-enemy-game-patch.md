**[Naruto Slugfest](https://jayagonoy.com/naruto-slugfest-now-available-on-google-play-and-app-store/) has a new patch update,** local publisher Cubizone said in a press release sent to _keepsakes._

The new content and update of the game patch includes an additional **Chapter 7 (titled "The Unseen Enemy")**, following the battle between Naruto, Pain and other Naruto Shippuden Characters.

## Naruto Slugfest patch's new features

Included as well in the update is the increased Level Cap, allowing players to raise their stats up to Level 70 through multiple quests.

Multiple PvP and PvE features are launched with the update, like Avalanche which allows Players to challenge their PvP prowess in a Cross Server Ranking Event. Players can also try other features such as Assault Drill and Partner Trial.
