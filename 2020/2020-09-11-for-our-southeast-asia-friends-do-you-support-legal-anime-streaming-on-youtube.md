Just a few days ago, I got a message from the [Kurotsuki Online Convention](https://jayagonoy.com/online-anime-con-weekend-kurocon-funimationcon-anime-expo-lite-aniplex-online-fest-july-3-5-2020/) (KuroCon) team that my application for a fan panel has been approved, and I'm coordinating with the team to deliver my presentation for KuroCon volume 2 coming this September 26-27.

As part of the presentation, I am looking for anime fans in Southeast Asia to help me with my independent research. What I'm looking for are anime fans around the region of any age to answer a series of brief questions regarding the use of the YouTube platform in legal anime distribution.

Currently, the active participants in the survey are from the Philippines, but I'm also seeking for participants from other countries within the Southeast Asia region.

You can participate by filling up the survey below. Terms and Conditions are also indicated in the form just before you fill it up.

I absolutely hope for your active participation. You can share this with your friends!
