After all that has been said and done, we can't deny that ABS-CBN as an institution has been home to some experiences of mine.

One of the memories I had with its broadcast complex, the ELJ Communication Center, is Restaurant 9501, the company's corporate dining facility at 14th floor.

As a home for the media people, 9501 is a host to many press conferences. I think that the restaurant put the spotlight on what would be the bloggers' conferences (blogcons).

These happen when Deremoe was still alive, active, high and wild.

## Hero Face-Off 2014
