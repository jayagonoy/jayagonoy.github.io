For one month, the National Capital Region (Metro Manila) is placed under quarantine. Prior to this, events have been postponed left and right ([as posted on our COVID-19 Live Blog](https://jayagonoy.com/covid19-diary-live-blog/)) - not only in the Philippines but also overseas.

In the Philippines, there is a plan for an "Online Cosplay Convention" where cosplayers share cosplay trials with the use of social media.

https://www.facebook.com/GZEY18/posts/4235097866507777

What started as a simple meme will become an actual activity for Cosplayers tonight at 8pm, March 18th.

https://www.facebook.com/HiroTan.Jaguar/posts/3235926953088792

Cosplayers are advised to post their cosplay trials on Facebook at 8:00pm with the hashtag #OnlineCosplayConvention:

> 1\. Tommorow (march 18 @ 8pm) everyone will go on full cosplay trial.
> 
> 2\. Post a picture or Go FB live or greet your friends!
> 
> 3\. Flood the newsfeed of your friends with your cosplays and spread POSITIVITY during the time of quarantine!
> 
> 4\. Additional: use the hashtag

This live blog is set up to curate posts from Cosplayers who will be participating. We will also be [Live on Facebook](http://facebook.com/jayagonoyweb) by this time.
