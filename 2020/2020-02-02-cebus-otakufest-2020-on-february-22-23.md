Recently, [Netflix got the rights to release 21 Studio Ghibli films in most territories](https://media.netflix.com/en/press-releases/netflix-releases-21-studio-ghibli-masterpieces-around-the-world). Speaking of Ghibli, what's the next event to have that kind of theme? **Cebu's Otakufest**.

I am returning to [Cebu](https://jayagonoy.com/i-tried-cebus-local-fastfood-for-a-change-e54ad3c09f31/) [for](https://jayagonoy.com/cebu-city-otaku-journey-2019-23be5ad5a9a2/) [the](https://jayagonoy.com/archcon-2019-event-feature/) fourth time to witness this year's **Otakufest**, happening at the **Maze Garden of SM Seaside City Cebu** on the weekend of February 23-24, 2020.

Previously, the venue is stated to be held at UP Cebu in Lahug, but due to the recent outbreak and scare of COVID-2019, the Otakufest team made big leaps to proceed with holding the event.

[Read their official announcement](https://web.facebook.com/ofest/posts/2527021524068552)

https://www.facebook.com/ofest/videos/955281604870899/
