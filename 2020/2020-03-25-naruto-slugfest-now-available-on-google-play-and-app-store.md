Our friends at [Cubizone](/keywords/cubizone/) has informed us that their latest licensed mobile game [Naruto Slugfest](https://jayagonoy.com/mobile-game-naruto-slugfest-introduced-in-the-philippines/) is now on Open Beta Testing, and you can get the game on Android and iOS, two months after they told us about the [Closed Beta Testing](https://jayagonoy.com/naruto-slugfest-cbt-now-live/).

Based on Masashi Kishimoto's popular "Naruto Shippuden" title, Naruto Slugfest is dubbed as the world's first 3D Open World Action MMORPG mobile game which featured 3D cinematic visuals and an environment that resembles the world of Naruto.

Players in Southeast Asia (Indonesia, Thailand, Singapore, Malaysia, Philippines, Cambodia, Laos, Myanmar, Brunei, Timor-Leste), India, and Australia can now enjoy the game on their smartphones.

The game's plot starts in the Naruto Shippuden timeline where Naruto comes back to the Konoha Village after a two-year-long training with Jiraiya. At the same time, the Five Shinobi Nations are now facing the biggest threat as the Akatsuki starts to become more active. 

Aside from coming back from training, Naruto must also deal with the loss of someone he considers a brother—Sasuke, who joins Orochimaru in exchange for power in order to have his revenge on his brother Itachi. ​​
