**Aniplus Asia** secures big anime titles for its Fall 2020 simulcast: **Is It Wrong to Try to Pick Up Girls in a Dungeon? III**, **Fire Force Season 2**, **HYPNOSISMIC-Division Rap Battle- Rhyme Anima**, **The Irregular at Magic High School: Visitor Arc**, **Our Last Crusade or the Rise of a New World**, **Hayate no Gotoku!**, **TONIKAWA: Over The Moon For You** and **D4DJ: First Mix**.

When this has been emailed to me, I quickly replied, "Nice lineup we have," because it really is, according to my anime taste, a nice lineup.

Imagine dreaming about a rap battle in a fire dungeon complete with DJs lights, MCs and all. You are reppin' the visiting team on its last crusade, and the topic to rap about was... love life.

Yeah, I made that up - let's get straight to the anime channel's offerings for the last three months of the year. Do take note that I'll show the date/time of airing as Aniplus Asia states it, followed by my translation of their date/time slots for those who are used to the AM/PM time format.
